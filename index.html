Meet Betheney Urquhart

♫

Welcome! I'm Betheney Urquhart, a passionate piano teacher dedicated to introducing young children to the wonderful world of music.

With years of experience working specifically with children aged 4 and above, I understand that every child learns differently. My approach combines structured technique with creative play, games, and storytelling to keep young minds engaged and excited about learning.

I believe that starting music early builds confidence, improves concentration, and gives children a gift they'll treasure for life.

  • Specialist in early childhood music education
  • Warm, patient, and encouraging teaching style
  • Lessons tailored to each child's pace and interests
  • Current Working with Children clearance

Lesson Programs

Fun, structured programs designed for young learners

🎴

Tiny Tots (Ages 4-5)

A playful introduction to the piano using songs, games, rhythm activities, and colour-coded notes. 20-minute sessions designed for little attention spans.

  • 20-minute lessons
  • Learning through play
  • Basic rhythm & note recognition
  • Fun sticker reward charts
Most Popular
🎹

Young Musicians (Ages 6-9)

Building solid foundations with proper technique, music reading, and a mix of classical pieces and popular songs children love.

  • 30-minute lessons
  • Reading music notation
  • Technique & posture
  • Recital opportunities
🎶

Rising Stars (Ages 10+)

For more advanced young players ready to tackle graded examinations, performances, and a wider repertoire of musical styles.

  • 30-45 minute lessons
  • Exam preparation available
  • Theory & composition
  • Performance confidence
